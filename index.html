Shipping Company Data Management System

Project Description

In our project, we are going to design a shipping company data management system. This system will store information about customers, shipments, employees (including couriers), branches, complaint reports, and cargo trailers. It will help the company to track the shipments. Also, by using this system the company will be able to get informed about customer complaints.
